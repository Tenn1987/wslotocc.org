Welcome

The West Side Lake of the Ozarks Chess Club is a local, in-person chess club welcoming players of all skill levels.

We host regular meetups, casual games, monthly tournaments, and an annual championship series.

Upset of the Day Calculation
Awarded to the player achieving the largest rating upset based on published USCF ratings at event start. Wins count the full rating difference; draws count half (difference ÷ 2). In case of a tie on upset score, a win takes precedence over a draw.
